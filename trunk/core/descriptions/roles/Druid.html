The Druid is a spellcaster using his or her deep connection with nature as a source of power for spells. Druids have access to a mix of defensive and healing spells, with some offensive spells as well.
Wisdom is the most important ability score for all Druids, as it controls all aspects of their spellcasting. Dexterity is also important for increasing the Druid's modest Armor Class and improving attack rolls with ranged weapons.
